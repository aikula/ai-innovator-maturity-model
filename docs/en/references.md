# References and Related Work

AI-Innovator Maturity Model v0.1 is an original synthesis, but it is connected to several existing research and product directions.

## Related directions

- AI capability taxonomies and discussions around future AI levels.
- Agentic AI systems for task execution and workflow automation.
- Self-play and iterative improvement systems.
- Generative agents and synthetic social environments.
- Agent-based simulation of markets and organizations.
- Synthetic personas and synthetic market research.
- AI-assisted scientific hypothesis generation.
- Business model innovation and category design.
- Monte Carlo simulation and scenario planning.

## Positioning

This model combines these directions into a practical enterprise framework for evaluating AI systems that create, test, simulate, and learn from innovation hypotheses.

The model does not claim that current AI systems already provide general innovation capability. It defines maturity levels for systems moving from basic idea generation toward self-learning market labs and business model innovation.
